## Section Subnav Rules

- Mining pages show `Mining`, `Main Pool`, `Nano Pool`, `Stats`, and `Docs`.
- Wallet page shows `Login`, `Register`, `Dashboard`, `Deposit`, `Withdraw`, `Transactions`, and `Support`.
- Explorer/Stats pages show `Explorer`, `Stats`, `Launch Reserve`, and `Burn Tracker`.
- Docs pages show quick links to setup, pools, wallet risk, reserve, FAQ, and support.
